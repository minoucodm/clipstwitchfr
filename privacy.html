Privacy Policy — ClipsTwitchFR

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ClipsTwitchFR ("the App"), operated by Antoine, France, handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

ClipsTwitchFR collects only the minimum information necessary:

  • TikTok OAuth access tokens to publish videos on your behalf
  • TikTok user ID (user.info.basic) to identify the authenticated account
  • Twitch channel names to monitor chat activity

2. How We Use TikTok Data

We use the following TikTok API scopes:

  • user.info.basic — used solely to verify the identity of the authenticated TikTok user
  • video.upload — used solely to publish Twitch clip videos on the authenticated TikTok account

We do not use TikTok data for any other purpose, including advertising or analytics.

3. Data Storage

All data including access tokens is stored locally on your device only. ClipsTwitchFR does not transmit or store any personal information on external servers.

4. Third Party Services

  • TikTok API — for video publishing
  • Twitch API — for stream and chat monitoring
  • Cloudinary — for temporary video hosting before publishing
  • OpenAI Whisper — for automatic subtitle generation (runs locally)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can revoke ClipsTwitchFR's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Connected Apps".

7. Contact

For any questions: contact@clipstwitchfr.com
